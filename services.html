Our Services


Tunken Elevators is your trusted source for all elevator sales and services.
We specialize in providing top-of-the-line elevator systems and superior customer
service, so you can have peace of mind that your elevator needs will be met. We offer
reliable elevator services for residential and commercial clients. Our experienced
technicians can provide installation, maintenance, and repair services to keep your
elevator running smoothly and safely. We also provide complete modernization
services to upgrade your elevator system to the latest technology. We are dealers of
Movilift from Italy & BSB Asansor elevator from Turkey. We sell, install & maintain
a full range of elevators which includes passenger elevators, freight elevators, car
elevators, residential elevators, vertical platform lifts, hospital elevators and all
associated vertical transportation equipment by various specifications.
